Marcello in the United States
A. Reading
Six years ago, Marcello went to the United States. He dreamed of becoming a fluent English speaker. He took beginner classes in San Francisco. Reading was easy, but he struggled with grammar. None of his classmates spoke Italian.
After a month, Marcello’s classes got easier. His English improved and soon he could have long conversations. He only spoke English with his classmates. They watched Hollywood movies after class. His dream of speaking fluently came true!
