# 📝 ESL Lesson Plan: Going to the Gym

## 🎯 Expected Goals
1. **Vocabulary Building:** Master gym-related vocabulary and phrases (`work out`, `cardio`, `weight lifting`).
2. **Grammar & Word Choice:** Practice constructing complete sentences and using correct word choices when describing daily routines and exercise habits.

## ⏱️ Pacing & Rhythm (50 mins total)
*   **0-10m:** Greeting, Small Talk, and reviewing any past corrections.
*   **10-20m (Slide 1):** Topic introduction via Warm-up questions (focusing on exercise habits).
*   **20-35m (Slides 2 & 3):** Vocabulary introduction and Sentence Pattern Learning (`If..., you should...`).
*   **35-45m (Slides 4 & 5):** Simplified reading comprehension based on the dialogue, and guided speaking practice discussing gym habits.
*   **45-50m (Slide 6):** Wrap-up, feedback, and homework assignment.

## 🔑 Customization Strategy
*   Because Leon's goal is Daily Conversation, the discussion questions are tailored to relate exercise to everyday life.
*   Since he struggles with grammar issues and word choices, the focus will be on encouraging him to express complete thoughts and providing gentle corrections.
*   The warm-up questions are designed to elicit descriptions of his current habits, addressing his "薄弱項" (Grammar Issues, Word Choices).

```markdown
# Slide 2

| Word | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **stationary bike** | an exercise bicycle that stays in one place |
| **spin class** | an exercise class on stationary bikes |
| **weight lifting** | exercise that focuses on making muscles stronger |
| **personal trainer** | a person who designs and supports your exercise plan |
| **the heads up** | useful information or a warning |
| **look into** | to search for more information about something |
| **registration** | signing up for a membership or event |
| **cardio** | exercise that gets your heart rate up (e.g., running, cycling) |
| **work out** (verb) | to exercise your body |
| **workout** (noun) | a single session of exercise |
```
